behavioral-diversity

(behavioral-diversity)

Returns the behavioral diversity of the population, as described by David Jackson in ‘Promoting phenotypic diversity in genetic programming’. It is the percent of distinct behavior vectors in the population. Since finite algebras has binary test cases, error vectors are equivalent to behavior vectors.
